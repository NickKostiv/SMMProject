You have certain rights in relation to personal data. If you have any requests related to your rights please contact us as described in section “HOW YOU CAN CONTACT US” below.

(a) Opt-out of targeted advertising

You can opt-out of interest-based advertising on mobile applications by checking the privacy settings of your Android or iOS device.

Apple devices:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Select Privacy
  3. Select Advertising and enable "Limit Ad Tracking"

Android devices:

  1. Open Settings
  2. Select Google
  3. Select Ads and enable "Opt out of Ads Personalization"

(b) Opt-out of marketing emails and other direct marketing

You may opt-out of receiving promotional communications from us, such as marketing emails, by following the instructions in such communications.
